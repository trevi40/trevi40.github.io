Extra Information

  1. Astoria is known for its Greek heritage, waterfront parks, and strong community bonds.
  2. Astoria Park features New York City's oldest and largest public swimming pool, which was used for the 1936 and 1964 Olympic trials.
  3. Astoria Park features a historic, 21,500-square-foot skate park located directly underneath the Triborough Bridge.
